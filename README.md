# Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard

**A simple and practical data science project to help a telecom company reduce customer loss.**

## What is this Project About?

This project analyzes customer data to predict **which customers might leave** the company (called "churn").

The goal is to help the company identify at-risk customers early and take action to keep them.

## What I Did

- Explored and visualized the customer data to find important patterns
- Created new useful features to improve predictions
- Built machine learning models to predict churn
- Explained why the model makes certain predictions
- Provided clear business recommendations

##  Key Findings

- Around **26.5%** of customers churned
- Customers on **month-to-month contracts** are much more likely to leave
- Customers with **high monthly bills** tend to churn more
- People without **Tech Support** or **Online Security** leave more often
- **New customers** are at higher risk of churning

##  Tools & Skills Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Machine Learning (XGBoost, Random Forest, Logistic Regression)
- Data Visualization
- Model Explainability (SHAP)

## Business Recommendations

- Offer discounts to encourage customers to choose longer contracts
- Provide special offers to customers with high monthly bills
- Promote Tech Support and Online Security services
- Improve support for new customers during their first few months
